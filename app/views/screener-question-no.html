You do not need an isolation note

You can only get an isolation note if:

  • you have been told to stay at home by official advice, NHS 111 or a healthcare professional

If you are not sure if you need to stay at home, get advice from:

  • England: NHS 111 online coronavirus service
  • Scotland: NHS inform
  • Wales: NHS Direct Wales
  • Northern Ireland: Call 111
