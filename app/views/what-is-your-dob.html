Why are we asking for your date of birth?

We ask for your date of birth:

Your employer may want to verify your isolation note. Your date of birth helps identify you if there is more than one person with the same name where you work. Find out more about how employers can verify an isolation note [link]

Health authorities may use this data to learn about patterns of coronavirus in the UK. Individuals will not be identified. Find out more in our privacy policy [link to privacy policy].
